AirLenz is a software that provides real-time Air Quality Index

The function of this software is to provide real-time information about the Air Quality Index of various areas of Lahore City in Pakistan. It would also allow users to view statistics of various pollutants in the form of a report for data visualizations. The locality of information capturing sensors can also be viewed along with their statuses on the website.

We aim to provide reliable and real-time data for better monitoring of the environment.

Stake Holders

General Public

The general public can look at the AQI of any available region on the map. The selected region will be highlighted on the map and its AQI will be visible as well. The general public can also see any news alerts, notifications, and recent data on the same page. It can also download the statistical data of any time and location.

Health-care Sector

The researchers of the healthcare sector can use the data to monitor the polluted areas and study the possible causes in outbreak areas. They can work on individual pollutants and find the actual problems of the cause.

Ministry of Climate Change (MOCC)

These authorities can start their campaigns through our portal. They can look into the progress caused by the measures taken in any area to reduce the concentration of certain pollutants.

Our Objectives

Objectives of our software are as following:

  • 1. Develop a real-time monitoring system
  • 2. Monitoring the effectiveness of environmental campaigns
  • 3. Utilize TEIN network
Develop a real-time monitoring system for spatio-temporal mapping of harmful pollutants.
Use information from such a system for monitoring the effectiveness of climate mitigation and adaptation efforts.
Utilization of TEIN network. TEIN/PERN network will be used for the following objectives:


1. Data collection network.
2. Data analytics results and outcomes will be available for researchers, academicians, and the general public over TEIN/PERN network.
